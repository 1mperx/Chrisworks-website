About ChrisWorks

We're not just a studio — we're a team of childhood friends who grew up building on Roblox. Now we turn that passion into results for game creators worldwide.

Our Story

ChrisWorks was born from a simple idea: what if a group of lifelong Roblox players used their deep knowledge of the platform to help other creators succeed? We started playing Roblox as kids, building games for fun and learning the engine inside out. Over the years, we noticed that many talented developers had great games but lacked the technical expertise or time to optimize them for growth.

That's when we decided to turn our passion into a business. Today, ChrisWorks is a full-service game development studio that partners with Roblox creators to scale their games to the front page. We combine deep analytics knowledge, hands-on development skills, and a genuine love for the platform.

Our Mission

We believe every great game deserves great results. Our mission is to bridge the gap between creative vision and technical execution, helping developers reach millions of players by optimizing every aspect of their game — from retention mechanics to monetization, from UI/UX to backend infrastructure.

Our Values

Results First
