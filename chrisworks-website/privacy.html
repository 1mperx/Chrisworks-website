Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

ChrisWorks ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Contact Form Data: When you fill out our contact form, we collect your name, email address, game link, and message. This data is processed by Formspree (our form handling service) and forwarded to us via email.
  • Career Applications: If you apply for a position, any information you share via Discord is handled through Discord's platform and their respective privacy policy.
  • Automatic Data: We do not use cookies or tracking scripts. We do not collect analytics data on this website.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information you provide through our contact form is used exclusively to:

  • Respond to your inquiry
  • Evaluate potential partnerships
  • Communicate with you about our services

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services:

  • Formspree: Handles our contact form submissions. Their privacy policy can be found at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.
  • Google Fonts: Used to load typefaces. Google may collect basic connection data. Their privacy policy is at policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Vercel: Our hosting provider. Their privacy policy is at vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy.

5. Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained only for the duration necessary to respond to your inquiry and evaluate a potential partnership. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our contact form or reach out on Discord at @chris_devs.
